Balicaocao Highland Resort

Gently nestled on the hill more than 500 feet above sea level, Balicaocao offers perfect ambiance for rest and leisure while taking a breathtaking view and panorama of the coastal towns of Southern Negros Occidental, the blue waters of Panay Gulf and the Island-Province of Guimaras. It has one pavilion, cottages, swimming pool, and cooking area perfect for family picnics.
